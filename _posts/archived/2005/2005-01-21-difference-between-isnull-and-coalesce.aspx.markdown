What will the last two lines print. Will they be the same?

```sql
DECLARE @test VARCHAR(2)
DECLARE @first VARCHAR(4)
DECLARE @second< VARCHAR(4)

SELECT @first = ISNULL(@test, 'test')
SELECT @second = COALESCE(@test, 'test')

PRINT @first
PRINT @second
```
